Trust the Whisper

The Hundred Story Home leads you on an inspirational journey that begins with a question, "Where are the beds?" and ends with over one hundred formerly homeless people living in homes of their own.

Author, Kathy Izard, was enjoying a comfortable existence when she came face to face with the reality that while her dedicated service at the community soup kitchen might be feeding her soul, it would never solve the bigger needs of the homeless population. Confronting the question of where are the beds? Kathy realized that she had been called to take on what seemed like an insurmountable task﹘to build housing for Charlotte's homeless.

Woven together with this uplifting story of a call to social action is Kathy's personal struggle with faith, forgiveness and fulfillment. In telling her story, Kathy invites you to consider rewriting your own. What's calling you? And as crazy at it seems, it may be crazier not to try.

    Questions?


    Why can’t I order The Hundred Story Home on Amazon or buy in my local books store anymore?
    The Hundred Story Home was originally a self-published book released October 2016. Harper Collins Christian/Thomas Nelson Division has purchased the book and will be releasing a new expanded edition in paperback, eBook and audio book beginning June 2018. You can pre-order your copy now!

    If I have a copy will the new version be different?
    If you liked The Hundred Story Home, you will love the new version! Harper Collins will be creating a new expanded version with some new content, Reader’s Guide and even photos to further bring the stories alive for readers. They have even purchased translation rights and film rights so stay tuned!

    Why change the book from the original version?
    As a self-published book, it was difficult to get The Hundred Story Home into bookstores throughout the country and in formats like eBook and audiobook. Collaborating with a leading industry publisher like Harper Collins Christian allows The Hundred Story Home to be available nationwide and in many formats rather than simply paperback.

    How soon can I have it?
    The new version of The Hundred Story Home will be available June 2018. pre-order and you will be among the first to receive a copy!
