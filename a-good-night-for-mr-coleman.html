A New Book to Inspire Kids to Dream Big and Do Good!

A Good Night for Mr. Coleman is a feel-good story that teaches children about the power of doing good. Told through whimsical illustrations, young Grace helps a homeless friend she meets in a soup kitchen to finally live in a home of his own. Based a on true story, this book helps explain the complex concept of homelessness and encourages readers of all ages to Believe in Something Bigger than Ourselves.

Synopsis

Grace loves working with her family in the local soup kitchen and helping homeless Neighbors like Mr. Coleman. But when she learns Mr Coleman doesn't have a home with a bed, Grace listens to a whisper that she must do something about it. This inspiring tale based on a true story will help you believe in your dreams too!
